Nammude vipani

"Naadan Vipani"/ "Nammude Vipani" is a remarkable e-commerce platform that emerged as a swift and collaborative response to the challenges posed by the pandemic season in 2021. Developed and deployed within an astonishingly short span of 7 days, this platform represents a harmonious collaboration between key stakeholders: the Agriculture Department of the PTA District, HortiCorp, and VFPCK.

The user-friendly design, exemplified by the simple OTP-based login, makes the platform easily accessible to a wide range of users. The platform's offerings of fresh vegetables and commodities can be conveniently browsed and purchased from the comfort and safety of one's home.

In a mere 7 days since its launch, NaadanVipani has warmly embraced over 500 middle-aged and elderly customers who often encounter technological barriers. This rapid adoption owes itself to the platform's intuitive user experience and uncomplicated interface, ensuring accessibility and comfortable navigation for this demographic.

Tech Stack & Architecture

The chosen tech stack includes:

Frontend: Developed with React for an engaging user interface.

Backend: Powered by PHP to manage server-side logic and functions.

Database: Utilizing MySQL to efficiently store and manage data.

Authentication/SMS: Incorporating Google authentication and SMS integration for user security.

Server: AWS EC2 serves as the robust and scalable hosting environment. This comprehensive tech stack, coupled with AWS EC2 as the server, contributes to a seamless user experience and efficient platform operations.
